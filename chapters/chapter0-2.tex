\chapter{Background and Related Work}
\label{ch:background-and-related-work}

This chapter provides the theoretical and methodological foundation of the thesis, covering the evolution of \ac{NLP} tools and their applications to social media data. It explores the challenges of processing noisy, heterogeneous text from platforms like Twitter and discusses existing work on key areas such as hate speech detection, misinformation analysis, and multilingual topic classification. By situating the thesis within the broader research landscape, this chapter demonstrates how the presented contributions advance the state of the art.
